Theory Sessions

  • Instant vs Brewed Coffee
  • Fresh roast vs old roast

  • Basic types of coffee and beans
  • Parts of the machine and barista tools

Practical Sessions

  • Leveling
  • Tamping
  • Espresso shot extraction
  • Perfecting an espresso
  • Milk frothing for cappuccino
  • Perfecting a cappuccino
  • 12 steps from bean to cup
