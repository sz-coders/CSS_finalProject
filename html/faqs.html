FAQs

Any Question? Here is the Answer!

Is espresso stronger than drip coffee?

Per ounce, espresso is more concentrated; per typical serving the total caffeine may be similar or lower depending on serving sizes.

How should I store coffee beans?

Keep beans in an airtight container, away from light, heat, and moisture. Grind just before brewing for best freshness.

Does coffee dehydrate?

Moderate coffee intake does not cause dehydration; it contributes to daily fluid intake.

Which has more caffeine — dark or light roast?

Light roast has slightly more caffeine by weight, but the difference is very small.

What is single-origin coffee?

Single-origin coffee comes from one specific region or farm, highlighting unique flavors.

Cold brew vs iced coffee?

Cold brew is steeped in cold water for hours and is smoother. Iced coffee is hot brewed coffee poured over ice.

Which coffee bean is strongest?

Robusta contains the most caffeine, while Arabica is smoother and more aromatic.
